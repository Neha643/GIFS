Grapes come in different colors and forms.
There are red, green, and purple grapes, seedless grapes, grape jelly, grape jam and grape juice, raisins, currents, and sultanas, not to mention wine.
Grapes are also a popular finger food. The nutrients in grapes offer a number of possible health benefits.
