A banana is an elongated, edible fruit.
The fruits grow in clusters hanging from the top of the plant.
Bananas contain fiber, potassium, folate, and antioxidants, such as vitamin C.
A 2017 review found that people who follow a high fiber diet have a lower risk of cardiovascular disease than those on a low fiber diet.
Bananas are a healthy source of fiber, potassium, vitamin B6, vitamin C, and various antioxidants and phytonutrients.
Many types and sizes exist. Their color usually ranges from green to yellow, but some varieties are red.
